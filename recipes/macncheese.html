Mac and Cheese Recipe

Go Back to Index

Description:

This creamy, cheesy mac and cheese is the ultimate comfort food. With a rich, velvety sauce and perfectly cooked pasta, it's a simple yet delicious dish that's sure to satisfy. Top with a golden, crispy breadcrumb crust for the perfect finishing touch!

For extra flavor, try adding a pinch of garlic powder, a sprinkle of smoked paprika, or mixing in some cooked bacon. Whether you serve it as a side or enjoy it as a main dish, this mac and cheese is a crowd-pleaser every time!

Ingredients Required:

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps to make mac and cheese:

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese at 350 degrees F until the topping is golden brown.
